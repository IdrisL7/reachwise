# GetSignalHooks — How It Works (Product Demo Video Script)

> **Format:** Screen recording with voiceover. ~3–4 minutes.
> **Tone:** Confident, direct, no hype. Show the product doing what it claims.
> **Tools:** Record with Loom/Descript, voiceover with ElevenLabs or Descript AI voice.

---

## INTRO (0:00–0:15)

**[SCREEN: Homepage hero section]**

**VOICEOVER:**
"GetSignalHooks generates evidence-backed outbound from any company URL. Every hook cites a real source — quote, title, and date. No invented claims. Let me show you how it works."

## SCENE 1: Generate Hooks (0:15–1:00)

**[SCREEN: Scroll to demo section. Type "shopify.com" into the URL field.]**

**VOICEOVER:**
"Paste a company URL. Optionally pick a role — VP Sales, RevOps, Founder — so the hooks match how that buyer thinks."

**[SCREEN: Select "VP Sales" from the role dropdown. Click "Generate hooks". Show loading, then results appearing.]**

**VOICEOVER:**
"In a few seconds you get three hooks, each with a different angle. Trigger — what changed. Risk — what breaks if they ignore it. Tradeoff — the decision they're weighing."

**[SCREEN: Hover over the first hook card. Point out the badges: angle, confidence, Tier A, Role: VP Sales.]**

**VOICEOVER:**
"Every hook shows its angle, confidence level, evidence tier, and the role it's written for. Tier A means first-party or reputable source. Tier B is weaker evidence — we label those as verification hooks and phrase them as questions, not claims."

**[SCREEN: Scroll to evidence panel below the hook. Highlight the quote, source title, and date.]**

**VOICEOVER:**
"And here's the receipt. The actual quote from the source, the title, and when it was published. Your SDR can defend every line they send."

## Production Notes

- **Total runtime:** ~3:00–3:30
- **Screen resolution:** Record at 1920x1080, browser zoomed to 110% for readability
- **Mouse movements:** Slow, deliberate. Hover on elements you're describing.
- **Pauses:** 1-second pause between scenes for visual breathing room
- **Music:** Optional low ambient background, drop volume during voiceover
- **Suggested AI voice:** ElevenLabs "Adam" or "Josh" — professional, not overly energetic
- **Export:** MP4, 1080p. Upload to YouTube (unlisted or public) and embed on site.
